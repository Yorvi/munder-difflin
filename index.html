ABOUT US

Here at Munder Difflin we are a small paper company where our best product isn’t so much paper as it is customer service. That is what sets Munder Difflin apart from our competitors.

Based in Scranton, PA, Munder Difflin may just be two rooms in a warehouse, but what we lack in flash we make up for in Hardwork, Loyalty, and most importantly Professionalism.

It all starts with an idea. But you can never tell where an idea will end up. Because ideas spread, they change, grow. They connect us with the world, and in a fast moving world, where good news moves at the speed of time, and bad news isn’t always what it seems. Because when push comes to shove, we all deserve a second chance, to score.
